Welcome to Honors Logistics

HONORS LOGISTICS LIMITED was started and incorporated in 2016. Its main objective was to provide hardware logistical support to construction companies but has since ventured into Building and Civil Engineering Construction, Road Works, Water Works and Electrical Services

Since inception the company has offered several technical services with a sole mission of ensuring safe working environment with reasonable cost, quality and according to the schedule. We are also registered with National Construction Authority, NCA. The personnel are rich in various experiences in their professional fields. These fields are the aforesaid Construction and specialized Construction/Engineering services.

More about us

1. Our Vision

To be the leader in construction industry, consultants and management contracting services in Kenya and East Africa.

2. Our Mission

To provide quality services to our esteemed customers with competitive market price.

3. Our Objectives

  • 1. To become an established company in construction, consulting and Engineering.
  • 2. To be a client oriented company that operates, satisfies and exceeds customer expectations.
  • 3. To remain fully compliant with International Industry and occupational health, safety and environmental standards.

4. Our Strengths

  • 1. Our staff members have vast experience in the area of electrical Engineering works construction works.
  • 2. Conformation to prerequisites and laws governing business in Kenya and East Africa.
  • 3. The infusion of visionary leadership into the running of company gives us a competitive edge over our competitors in the sector.
  • 4. Being strict on safety and health of our team members, we are always assured of quality to our client organizatio.
